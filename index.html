This is a strategy game for two players. Players take turns placing tokens of their colour in the grid. Tokens fall if there is nothing beneath them. If a line of four tokens of your colour is made at any point, they vanish and you get a point. A line of five scores two points, six scores three, and seven scores four. The game ends when the board is full.

You should adjust your browser zoom until the whole board is on the screen.
